# References

A curated list of resources I used while writing these notes. Read them in roughly this order if you want to go deeper.

---

## Foundational paper

- **Attention Is All You Need** - Vaswani et al., 2017
  [https://arxiv.org/abs/1706.03762](https://arxiv.org/abs/1706.03762)
  The original Transformer paper. Section 3 (Model Architecture) and Section 3.2 (Attention) are the parts you want. Everything in this repo is a friendlier explanation of what is in this paper.

---

## Articles - GeeksforGeeks

- **Getting Started with Transformers**
  [https://www.geeksforgeeks.org/getting-started-with-transformers/](https://www.geeksforgeeks.org/getting-started-with-transformers/)
  High-level overview of Transformer architecture, encoder-decoder structure, and use cases.

- **Self-Attention in NLP**
  [https://www.geeksforgeeks.org/self-attention-in-nlp/](https://www.geeksforgeeks.org/self-attention-in-nlp/)
  Walks through the Q/K/V mechanism with examples.

- **Word Embeddings in NLP**
  [https://www.geeksforgeeks.org/word-embeddings-in-nlp/](https://www.geeksforgeeks.org/word-embeddings-in-nlp/)
  Word2Vec, GloVe, fastText, and contextual embeddings compared.

- **Bag of Words (BoW) Model in NLP**
  [https://www.geeksforgeeks.org/bag-of-words-bow-model-in-nlp/](https://www.geeksforgeeks.org/bag-of-words-bow-model-in-nlp/)
